## 6-Month Roadmap
**Version 2.4**

**Goal by End of Month 6:**  
25–30 paying customers + **€15k–€30k MRR**

## ✅ Delivered so far (product build — ahead of schedule)

The full product has been built well ahead of the Month 2–3 plan. Shipped and verified:

**Core platform**
- ✅ Auth (email/password, Google sign-in, magic link, password reset) with hardening: typo guard, brute-force lockout, remember-me, session-expiry handling, custom SMTP (Resend)
- ✅ Onboarding + compliance dashboard + health score
- ✅ Controls (owner / due date / notes) + continuous monitoring & alerts
- ✅ Evidence vault (upload, signed-download, delete)
- ✅ AI Policy Generator + AI Compliance Co-Pilot (Groq)
- ✅ Public Trust Center + team invites (multi-user, role-based)

**Compliance frameworks (5)** — SOC 2, ISO 27001, **HIPAA, GDPR, PCI DSS**

**Evidence integrations (10)** — Google Workspace, GitHub, Slack, AWS, Okta, GitLab, Jira, Linear, Cloudflare, Google Cloud

**GRC modules**
- ✅ Vendor risk management
- ✅ **Risk Register** (likelihood × impact, treatment, status)
- ✅ **Employee training tracker** (assignment, completion, overdue alerts)
- ✅ **Predictive alerts** (deadlines approaching, readiness pace)
- ✅ **One-click audit report** (print / save-as-PDF)

**Commercial**
- ✅ Stripe billing — Starter / Growth / Enterprise tiers, **monthly + annual**, **founder discount** (promotion codes)
- ✅ Legal pages (Privacy, Terms) + signup consent

**Remaining to launch:** public deploy (Vercel + domain), then Product Hunt / LinkedIn launch. Deeper ML-based prediction and a training content library are future enhancements.
