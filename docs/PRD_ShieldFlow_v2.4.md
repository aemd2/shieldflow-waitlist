## 1. Vision

ShieldFlow is an **AI-first Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)** platform that delivers the core value of expensive tools like Vanta, Drata, and OneTrust — at **60-80% lower price**.

Built specifically for **11–200 employee** SaaS, fintech, and healthtech companies who are tired of overpaying for compliance tools that are too complex and bloated.

## 5. MVP Scope (Realistic First Version)

### Core Features (Build These First)

- Automated evidence collection (start with 8–10 popular tools)
- Continuous monitoring & alerts
- **AI Compliance Co-Pilot** (chat assistant)
- Policy & procedure generator
- Basic dashboard + compliance health score
- Simple Trust Center / Security page

### Later Features (After MVP)

- Predictive Risk Forecasting
- Vendor risk management
- One-click audit reports
- Employee training module
